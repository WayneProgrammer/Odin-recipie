Burger Recipe

Home

A classic American burger, delievered on two toasted buns, features a delicously grilled beef patty topped with tomato, fresh pickles, onions, lettuce and cheese. This recipe is all you need if you're craving a fuffiling and hearty meal!

Ingredients:

  • Ground beef (80/20 for juiciness)
  • Hamburger buns (toasted, sesame or plain)

    • Toppings:

  • American cheese slices
  • Lettuce (iceberg or romaine, shredded or whole leaves)
  • Sliced tomato
  • Pickles
  • Diced Onions
  • Ketchup
  • Mustard

Directions:

  1. Prepare the Patties: Divide the ground beef into 4 equal portions and season generously with salt and black pepper
  2. Preheat Grill or Pan: Heat a grill, griddle or skillet over medium-high heat.
  3. Cook the Patties:Placed the patties on the hot surface. Cook each side for 3-4 minutes. Add a slice of American cheese during the last minute of cooking; cover to melt.
  4. Toast the Buns: Lightly butter the buns and place them on the hot surface until golden brown
  5. Assemble the Burgers: Add the lettuce, pickles, tomato, onion, ketchup and mustard to your heart desires.
