Pizza Recipe

Home

Description:

Ahh.. the classic cheese pizza. When done right, nobody could go wrong with its bursting flavors of tomato and cheese. This recipe helps make you feel right at home with its comforting and inviting flavors, accompanied with its delicously soft, crust dough.

Ingredients:

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Olive oil
  • Fresh basil
  • Salt
  • Oregano
  • Garlic (optional)
  • Parmesan cheese (optional)
  • Your choice of toppings (pepperoni, mushrooms, bell peppers, onions, etc.)

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven: Set your oven to 475°F (245°C) and place a pizza stone or baking sheet inside to heat up.
  2. Prepare the dough: Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness.
  3. Add sauce: Spread an even layer of tomato sauce over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges.
  4. Add cheese: Sprinkle shredded mozzarella evenly over the sauce.
  5. Add toppings: Place your chosen toppings (pepperoni, vegetables, etc.) on top of the cheese.
  6. Bake the pizza: Transfer the pizza to the preheated stone or sheet and bake for 10–12 minutes, or until the crust is golden and cheese is bubbly.
  7. Garnish: Remove from the oven and add fresh basil, a drizzle of olive oil, or grated Parmesan if desired.
  8. Slice and serve: Let the pizza cool slightly, slice it up, and enjoy!
