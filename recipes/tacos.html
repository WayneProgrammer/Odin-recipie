Taco Recipe

Home

A classic taco is a flavorful handheld dish featuring seasoned meat—like beef, chicken, or pork-tucked into a warm corn or flour tortilla. It's topped with fresh ingredients like shredded lettuce, diced tomatoes, cheese, and a splash of salsa or lime, delivering a perfect bite of bold, zesty flavor.

Ingredients:

  • Ground beef, shredded chicken, or pork (seasoned)
  • Taco seasoning (cumin, chili powder, paprika, garlic powder, oregano, salt)
  • Corn or flour tortillas (soft or crispy)

    • Toppings:

  • Shredded lettuce
  • Diced tomatoes
  • Chopped onions
  • Shredded cheese (cheddar, Monterey Jack, etc.)
  • Fresh cilantro
  • Sliced jalapeños (optional)

Directions:

  1. Cook the Beef: Heat a skillet over medium heat. Add olive oil if needed. Add garlic and cook for 30 seconds. Add ground beef and break it up with a spatula. Cook until browned.
  2. Season: Stir in chili powder, cumin, paprika, oregano, cayenne, salt, and pepper. Add 1/4 cup water or broth. Simmer for 5 minutes until thickened.
  3. Warm the tortillasHeat tortillas in a dry skillet, microwave, or wrap in foil and warm in the oven
  4. Assemble the tacos: Spoon beef into tortillas. lettuce, tomatoes, cheese, onions, and your favorite toppings.
  5. Serve: Garnish with cilantro and a squeeze of lime.
